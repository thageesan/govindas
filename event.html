Host Your Event At Govinda's

Govinda's is a great place to host your party or events, corporate bookings, birthday parties and special occasions. Govinda's has a beautiful atmosphere with grand arches, lighting and art sculptures.

We can accommodate practically any size from 10 or more (Reservations required). For groups larger than 40, please contact our event coordinator for a special booking process.

E-mail govindastoronto@gmail.com or call 1-888-218-1040 for inquiries about reservations and hosting your events.

Catering

Do you want to have the Govinda's experience at another venue, your office or at home? We are able to cater to your specific needs. Choose from a variety of options from our famous Indian dishes, appetizers, salads, flatbreads, and desserts.

Please e-mail govindastoronto@gmail.com.
