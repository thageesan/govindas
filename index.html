Vegetarian / Vegan Restaurant Near Yorkville, Toronto

Govinda's is a vegetarian / vegan restaurant serving fusion cuisine located in an historic building in downtown Toronto, minutes from Yorkville.

Our aim is to provide fresh, nutritious vegetarian meals that delight the palate, promote good health, make the mind peaceful and elevate the consciousness. We offer an excellent dining experience in a casual and serene atmosphere.

All of our items are free of meat, fish and eggs and many of our dishes are dairy free and thus suitable for vegans as well. Everything at Govinda’s is prepared with the very best ingredients available and using the freshest produce.
